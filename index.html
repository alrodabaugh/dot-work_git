I create performances within the lineage of dance and choreography. My work includes found performance, dance generated from numerical scores, improvisation, and both found and written text, with a primary focus on framing. Pointing toward the performance of everyday interaction, mass spectacle, illegible manipulation and modes of disorientation and control, these performances question good faith and reveal manifestations of power.
